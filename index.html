CribzStats Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data the App handles, where it goes, and your choices. Short version: your datasets stay on your device; the only things that ever leave it are your account details, the computed summary numbers when you tap "Explain with AI", and a photo when you explicitly choose photo import.

A. Data we collect

Account data: your email address and a securely hashed password, managed by our authentication provider (Supabase). A profile record linked to your account. A record of the Terms version you accepted and when.

That is all. We do not collect analytics profiles, advertising identifiers, contacts, or location data, and we do not sell or rent any personal data to anyone.

B. Data that stays on your device

Your imported datasets (CSV, Excel, and photo-derived tables), your edits in the grid, all computed statistics, and all exported files are processed and stored on your device only. They are not uploaded to our servers and we cannot see them.

C. Data sent to the AI service, and when

Three features send data off your device, and all are triggered only by you:

1. Explain with AI: when you tap the button, the App sends the test name, the column names and types, and the already-computed result numbers (for example: t, df, p-value). It never sends your data rows. Our server enforces this: it forwards only a whitelist of summary fields.

2. Photo import: when you confirm the consent prompt, the photo you chose is sent so the AI can transcribe the table. Only that photo is sent, and only after you agree in the dialog.

3. Ask AI (test suggestions, typed commands, and in-app guidance): when you use the Ask AI box, the App sends your typed request plus a summary of your dataset consisting only of column names, detected types, and counts (row count, missing counts, and the number of distinct values). Cell values, including category labels, are never sent, and every suggested test runs on your device only after you approve it.

Both requests pass through our server (a Supabase Edge Function) to Google’s Gemini API, which processes them to produce the response. We do not use your data to train AI models. Google processes these requests under its own API terms.

D. Service providers

We use two processors: Supabase (account authentication, the profile database, and the server functions that proxy AI requests) and Google (the Gemini AI model that generates explanations and reads photographed tables). Each receives only what is described above, protected in transit by encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

E. How we use data

We use your account data to operate the Service: signing you in, keeping your session active, and securing the App. We use the AI request data solely to return the explanation or transcription you asked for. No advertising, no profiling, no sale of data.

F. Retention and deletion

Account data is kept while your account exists. Data on your device is under your control and disappears when you clear or uninstall the App. To delete your account and its data, email us at amstrong.mbibifli.kiawitech@gmail.com from your registered address; we will action the request within 30 days.

G. Security

Passwords are hashed by the authentication provider and never visible to us. Database access is protected by row-level security so an account can only read its own profile. AI service keys are held on the server, never inside the App. All network traffic is encrypted in transit. No system is perfectly secure, but we design so the sensitive path (your raw data) never leaves your device in the first place.

H. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you may withdraw consent for the photo feature at any time simply by not using it. Contact us at amstrong.mbibifli.kiawitech@gmail.com. If you believe we have not handled your data properly, you may also complain to the data protection authority competent for you.

I. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

J. Changes and contact

We may update this Privacy Policy; material changes will be flagged in the App and the version at the top will change. Questions, requests, or complaints: amstrong.mbibifli.kiawitech@gmail.com.
